Chapter I

Passage to Chult

From the House of Healing to the docks of Port Nyanzaru — by word, by storm, by blade, by ice.

6 sessions 10 fallen
Session I An Oath, a Map, and a Sending Stone — 1 Kythorn 15 MAR 2026
No blade was drawn. The company took Azlund's oath under zone of truth, received the map of Chult and fifty gold apiece, and boarded The Horizon at the Waterdeep docks.
Session II The Horizon, and the First Storm — 5 Kythorn 22 MAR 2026
Days aboard the Horizon: Chumble hid in a pickle-barrel and woke with a familiar named Wickles. The storm came on the fourth day; six hands met it, and every rope held.
Session III Blades in the Salt ✝ 4 10 Kythorn 29 MAR 2026
Sahuagin boarded at dusk. Corwin and Dragonbait held the prow; the company took the stern. Four fell to the party — blade, mockery, and bell. A flash of blue light behind a crate was not commented upon.
Session IV The Ring, Revealed — 28 Kythorn 12 APR 2026
An arm-wrestle unmasked no secrets, but words did. Harlen was revealed a Zhentarim stowaway and the cheese-thief. Corwin showed his left hand, told his name, and named the Ring of Winter.
Session V Through Pirate Steel, into Nyanzaru ✝ 6 29 Kythorn 19 APR 2026
Pirates boarded in the night. The deck ran red. When it became clear the company could not win the tally, Artus Cimber teleported them to Port Nyanzaru in a flash of ice and turned alone to the remaining crew.
Session VI Three Riders and a Ring — 30 Kythorn 26 APR 2026
The party rented three dinosaurs from the Tiryki Anchorage pens, crossed paths with Volo Geddarm on the march to Goldenthrone where they heard the history of Chult and bought his book, and ran the city race circuit — Lilac Mist took first place, and Greig finished third to claim Ubtao's Ring.
VThe Horizon

The Road Ahead

What is known, and where they turn.
What Is Known
  • The Ring of Winter — bound to Artus Cimber's hand, last seen opening an eye of frost on a deck of pirates.
  • The Soulmonger — engine of the Death Curse, somewhere in Chult.
  • The Death Curse — the dead will not stay raised, and the lately-raised waste in their flesh.
  • Azlund's Oath — fifty gold paid in hand, one rare gift each on return, if the Curse can be broken.
  • The Map of Chult — half-marked, given by Azlund at the Waterdeep docks.
  • The Sending Stone — Vex carries the pair, and Azlund waits at the other end.
  • Saja N'baza — a name in Chult, sought by Artus for word of his lost wife.
  • The Yuan-ti's Door — a yuan-ti drew blood and fled within; Wickles watches from the eaves.
Where They Turn

Port Nyanzaru is at their feet, and three guides offer the jungle road. Azlund's oath is half-kept, and Chult is still shoreless on the maps they have.

Those Who Stood With Them
  • Azlund of the House of Healing, whose oath bound the road
  • Tessa who walked the company down to the docks where the Horizon was moored

  • Captain Brennan veteran of the Nyanzaru run, who gave them a cloak of billowing for six ropes held
