## Tone reference

Saga fragment, gravestone epitaph, or the one-line caption under a
reliquary in a medieval chapel. Cool, compact, slightly elegiac. Not
triumphant, not comedic, not campy. Single-sentence, third person,
no "you" or "I". Verse ≤ ~12 words. Annotation ≤ ~14 words.
